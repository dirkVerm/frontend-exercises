COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectuous desease caused by a new virus.

This disease causes respiratory ilness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever and, in more severe cases, dificulty breathing.

You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face and avoiding close contact (1 meter of 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

How it spreads

Coronavirus disease spreads primarely through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose of mouth.

What are its symptoms.

The COVID-19 virus affects different people in different ways:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

COVID-19

There's currently no vaccine to prevent coronavirus disease. Take following precautions:

  1. Get Vaccinated and stay up to date on your COVID-19 vaccines
  2. Wear a mask
  3. Stay 6 feet away from others
  4. Avoid poorly ventilated spaces and crowds
